Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our handcrafted websites, process, and pricing.

How fast can my new website be ready?

We deliver fully functional, high-speed websites in just 5 to 7 business days once we receive your text, images, and brand details.

Are there any monthly subscription fees?

No! You pay a single upfront price for the design and code. You own the website 100% forever. The only ongoing costs are your domain and hosting, which usually cost a few euros per month.

Why are your websites faster than WordPress?

We build using clean, static Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS. Unlike WordPress, there are no heavy databases, bloated plugins, or slow page builders, resulting in instant loading and top PageSpeed scores.

Can guests book directly on the website?

Yes. We can seamlessly integrate direct contact/inquiry forms, WhatsApp instant booking buttons, or embed your existing Channel Manager / PMS booking engine (e.g., SabeeApp, Booking.com widget, Sirvoy).

Will my website work well on mobile phones?

Absolutely. We follow a strict Mobile-First approach. Since over 70% of travelers search for accommodations on smartphones, your site is optimized specifically for thumb navigation and fast mobile displays.

Can I edit the content myself later?

Since these are custom static websites built for speed and security, they don't rely on complex CMS dashboards. Small text or photo updates can be easily made via simple maintenance requests, or we can connect a lightweight headless CMS if needed.

What do I need to prepare before we start?

Just the basics: high-resolution photos of your property, your room details/amenities, pricing info, and any existing logo or branding materials you have. We take care of everything else!

How do we handle web hosting and domain?

If you already have a domain and hosting provider, we simply deploy the clean files there. If not, we help you set up an affordable, ultra-fast hosting account in your own name so you retain 100% control.
