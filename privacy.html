1. Overview

At FanStaticWeb, accessible from fanstaticweb.site, the privacy of our visitors and clients is one of our top priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information collected and how we use it.

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out our contact forms, reach out via WhatsApp, or send us an email. This may include:

  • Your name and job title/role

  • Information regarding your property (apartment, hotel, or pension details)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for business purposes, including:

  • Responding to your inquiries and providing custom website quotes

4. Web Analytics & Cookies

Because we build ultra-fast static websites, we minimize tracking. We use privacy-friendly, lightweight web analytics solely to monitor aggregated traffic metrics (such as visitor numbers and page views) without storing invasive third-party tracking cookies or profiling personal data.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical precautions to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal details to any third-party advertisers.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time regarding direct marketing communications
